## üõ† Setup Instructions
Once you have installed **effect-slack**, you will need to configure it for use with your Slack account.

1. **Create a Slack App**  
   - Go to the Slack API website ([api.slack.com/apps](https://api.slack.com/apps)).
   - Click on "Create New App" and choose your workspace.
   - Name your app and click "Create App".

2. **Set Up Permissions**  
   - Under "OAuth & Permissions," add the necessary permissions that your app will need (like sending messages).
   - Make sure to save your changes.

3. **Install to Workspace**  
   Click on "Install App" and authorize your new app to access your Slack workspace.

4. **Copy the Token**  
   In the "OAuth & Permissions" section, find and copy your **Bot User OAuth Token**. You will use this in the effect-slack application to authenticate.

5. **Input Token in effect-slack**  
   When you run effect-slack, it will prompt you to enter your Slack token. Paste the copied token here and press Enter.

6. **Begin Using It**  
   You‚Äôre ready to start sending messages! Use the interface to interact with your Slack workspace.
